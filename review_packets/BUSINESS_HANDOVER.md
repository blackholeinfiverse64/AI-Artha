## 5. Key Capabilities

### Financial Workflows
- **Double-Entry Accounting:** Every transaction is balanced
- **Hash-Chain Ledger:** Tamper-evident transaction history
- **Audit Trail:** Complete governance evidence for every action

### Governance
- **Capability-Based Access:** Each feature is governed by capability contracts
- **Decision Ledger:** Every governance decision is recorded
- **Provenance Chain:** Immutable evidence of all actions

### Compliance
- **GST Management:** Automated GST calculations and filing
- **TDS Management:** TDS deductions and challan tracking
- **Compliance Signals:** Automated alerts for compliance issues
